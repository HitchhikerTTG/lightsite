About iD

More than products -- a promise of confidence, care, and responsibility.

Our Mission

At iD, we believe managing incontinence is about more than just protection. It's about restoring confidence, dignity, and freedom. For over 45 years, we've been dedicated to listening, innovating, and improving the lives of millions worldwide.

What We Stand For

  • ✓ Human-Centered Care: We see every customer as an individual with unique needs and challenges.
  • ✓ Advanced Innovation: We use cutting-edge technology to deliver comfort, security, and performance.
  • ✓ Skin Health Commitment: Dermatologically tested materials to protect and care for sensitive skin.
  • ✓ Sustainability: We reduce waste, cut emissions, and source materials responsibly for a better planet.

Our Mood & Message

iD is empathetic, discreet, supportive, and empowering. We want every interaction with our brand -- from product design to packaging to customer care -- to reassure and uplift. We help people live fully, without compromise, and we stand beside caregivers, offering trust and expertise.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

  • ✓ Renewable Energy: 100% renewable electricity at all EU plants.
  • ✓ Responsible Materials: Certified and controlled wood-based raw materials.
  • ✓ Reduced Carbon: Optimized logistics and product design to lower emissions.
  • ✓ Less Plastic: Bags with at least 35% recycled content and reduced fossil-based plastics.

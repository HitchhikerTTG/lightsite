Step 1 -- Start with your everyday situation

Light leakage

Small leaks that happen now and then

  • When you laugh, sneeze or cough
  • During exercise or daily activities
  • You mainly want to feel dry and discreet

Medium leakage

Leaks you notice regularly

  • You need protection you can rely on
  • You may wear it for several hours
  • Feeling secure matters more than being ultra-thin

Night-time or heavier leakage

When protection needs to last longer

  • During the night or long rest periods
  • When changing is not always possible
  • You want maximum peace of mind

Step 2 -- Choose what feels right to wear

Pads

Discreet protection you change when needed

  • You prefer something light and flexible
  • You want to change protection during the day
  • You wear your own underwear
  • Available options include male-specific pads for a better fit

Pads are a good choice if you want control, discretion and easy changing.

Pants (pull-up underwear)

Protection that feels like regular underwear

  • You want an all-in-one solution
  • You move a lot or prefer a secure fit
  • You don’t want to think about changing during the day
  • Available in unisex sizes and discreet black options

Pants are ideal if comfort, confidence and freedom of movement matter most.

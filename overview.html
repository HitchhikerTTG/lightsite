Little discomforts

Do you sometimes loose a few drops of urine when you laugh, cough or sneeze, or do some physical exercise?

It is normal to sometimes have the urge to urinate, especially after having drunk a large quantity of fluids or coffee, or even after eating certain types of foods.
These discomforts are part of what is known as ‘stress incontinence’, because this occurs after coughing, laughing, sneezing or after physical exertion, which leads to a few drops leaking out.

BLADDER WEAKNESS


Do you sometimes have small losses and occasionally have accidents because you are unable to reach the toilet on time?

The sudden need to urinate and to go to the toilet immediately is known as bladder weakness or urge incontinence.
This causes little leaks, which can start to occur on a permanent basis. The leaks may be significant, with occasional or regular accidents. They generally occur during the day but can also happen at night.

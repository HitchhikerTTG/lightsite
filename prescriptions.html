Answering doubts

Do I need to send the original prescription?

You can send a copy first, and we'll let you know if we need the original. Most cases work with copies or digital uploads.

How long does approval take?

Usually 1-2 weeks. We'll keep you updated and can provide interim supplies if needed.

What if my prescription changes?

Just send us the updated prescription. We'll handle all changes with your insurance.

Will delivery show what's inside?

No. All packages are plain with no indication of contents.

Can I switch supplier later?

Yes, you can change suppliers at any time. There's no long-term commitment.
